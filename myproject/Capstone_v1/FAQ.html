Here are some of the most common questions that we get.

What types of services are offered on the marketplace?

Our platform offers for an exchange in numerous services including tutoring, delivery, and events.

How does the booking and payment process work?

You can book directly on this platform under the service you would like or you can directly email us at gwinteractive@support.com for booking inquiries. We accept payment in the form of Zelle and Venmo with this email.

How can I contact customer support for issues?

By simply emailing us at gwinteractive@support.com, we can ensure that your inquiries get resolved within minutes.

What safety measures are in place for users?

Users must provide a valid GWU email or GWU ID before proceeding forward on the platform exchange. This ensures direct verification of all users.

Can I upgrade my subscription plan?

Yes, you can upgrade your subscription plan at any time by contacting our team at gwinteractive@support.com
